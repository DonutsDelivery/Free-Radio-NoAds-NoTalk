## Notes on Stream URLs

1. **Direct URLs**: All URLs listed are direct stream URLs that can be used in media players like VLC, foobar2000, or internet radio devices.

2. **Format preference**:
   - MP3 streams have widest compatibility
   - AAC streams offer better quality at same bitrate
   - FLAC streams offer lossless quality but require more bandwidth

3. **Testing streams**: Stream URLs may change over time. If a stream doesn't work, check the station's official website for updated URLs.

4. **Quality tiers**:
   - 320 kbps: Audiophile quality (Linn, Naim)
   - 192-256 kbps: HiFi quality (France Musique, Concertzender)
   - 128 kbps: Standard quality (most stations)
   - 96 kbps and below: Mobile/low bandwidth
