## SomaFM Stations (Commercial-Free, Listener-Supported)

### SomaFM Folk Forward
- **Stream URL**: https://ice5.somafm.com/folkfwd-128-mp3
- **Bitrate**: 128 kbps MP3 (also available: 128 kbps AAC, 64 kbps AAC, 32 kbps AAC)
- **Sub-genres**: Indie folk, alt-folk, acoustic, singer-songwriter
- **Description**: Contemporary indie folk music ranging from softer acoustic sounds to harder-edged alt-folk, but always authentic. Features artists like Iron & Wine, Fleet Foxes, and emerging indie folk acts.
- **Location**: San Francisco, USA
- **Alt URLs**:
  - https://ice1.somafm.com/folkfwd-128-mp3 (backup)
  - https://ice5.somafm.com/folkfwd-128-aac (AAC 128k)

### SomaFM Suburbs of Goa
- **Stream URL**: https://ice5.somafm.com/suburbsofgoa-128-mp3
- **Bitrate**: 128 kbps MP3 (also available: 128 kbps AAC, 64 kbps AAC, 32 kbps AAC)
- **Sub-genres**: Indian, Asian fusion, desi beats, world electronic, global fusion
- **Description**: Desi-influenced Asian world beats and beyond. Features artists like Karsh Kale, Cheb I Sabbah, Nitin Sawhney, Kaya Project, TJ Rehmi, and Bombay Dub Orchestra.

## Notes on Stream URLs

- All URLs tested and verified from radio directories as of research date
- SomaFM and Radio Paradise are the most reliable 24/7 ad-free options
- FIP and Concertzender are European public broadcasters with consistent uptime
- Some Icecast/Shoutcast URLs may change - check radio-browser.info for updated URLs
- For best quality, prefer AAC streams when available (more efficient than MP3 at same bitrate)
