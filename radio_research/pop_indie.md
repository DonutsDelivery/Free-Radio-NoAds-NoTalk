## Notes

- **SomaFM** stations are highly reliable with multiple server redundancy. Using PLS or M3U links is recommended as they automatically select available servers.
- **KEXP** is considered one of the best indie radio stations globally, known for discovering new artists.
- **Radio Paradise** offers exceptional audio quality up to lossless FLAC streaming.
- **FIP Radio** is excellent for eclectic listening with heavy indie label representation.
- All stations listed are non-commercial, ad-free, and stream 24/7.

## Stream URL Formats

- `.mp3` - Standard MP3 stream (most compatible)
- `.aac` - AAC stream (better quality at same bitrate)
- `.pls` / `.m3u` - Playlist files that point to stream servers
- `-flac` suffix - Lossless FLAC streaming (highest quality)
