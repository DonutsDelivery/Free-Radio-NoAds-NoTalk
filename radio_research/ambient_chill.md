## Notes

### Alternative Stream Formats

Many stations offer multiple formats and bitrates:
- **SomaFM stations**: Also offer AAC-HE at 32kbps and 64kbps for low-bandwidth connections
- **Radio Paradise**: Offers FLAC lossless streams for audiophile-quality listening
- **Most stations**: Provide .pls and .m3u playlist files for easier player compatibility

### Recommended Players

These streams work with most media players that support HTTP streaming:
- VLC Media Player
- mpv
- Audacious
- Most hardware internet radios

### Station Reliability

- **SomaFM**: Highly reliable, multiple redundant servers (ice1-ice5)
- **Ambient Sleeping Pill**: Stable, single server
- **Echoes of Bluemars**: Community-maintained tribute station
- **Radio Paradise**: Very reliable, professional infrastructure
- **Chilltrax**: Non-profit community station
- **Nightwave Plaza**: Independent project, generally reliable
