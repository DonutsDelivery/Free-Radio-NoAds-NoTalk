## Notes on Stream URLs

- **PLS/M3U vs Direct URLs**: Playlist files (.pls, .m3u) are more stable and recommended by stations. Direct stream URLs may change over time.
- **Bitrate Options**: Higher bitrates (256-320 kbps) offer better audio quality but require more bandwidth.
- **Backup Servers**: Many stations offer alternate server URLs (ice1, ice4, ice5, etc.) for redundancy.
- **Protocol**: Most streams support both HTTP and HTTPS. Use HTTPS when available for security.
