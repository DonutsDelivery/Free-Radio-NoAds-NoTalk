## Notes

- All stations listed are free to stream and either ad-free or have minimal/no commercial interruptions

- For SomaFM, you can also use .pls playlist files (e.g., https://somafm.com/cliqhop.pls) which are more stable
- Higher bitrate = better audio quality but more bandwidth usage
- HTTPS streams are preferred for security and compatibility
